Paste flight data
+ what can I paste?

Flight blocks — paste Google Flights / Kayak output. Multi-leg trips and layovers are handled. Both single-line ("Kenya AirwaysEconomyBoeing 737KQ 427") and multi-line ("Air Tanzania" / "Economy" / "ATR 72PW 721") formats work, plus +1 / +2 day-offset times.

Plain-language commands — type instead of paste:

  • add hotel on jul 7 for znz hotel — 1-day lodging titled "znz hotel"
  • add lodging on jul 7 to jul 8 at riu palace — 2-day lodging
  • add activity on july 4 hiking
  • add location on jul 5 cape town
  • add flight on jul 6 jro to nbo — generic flights-lane placeholder (use a real Google Flights paste if you want times & TZ to flow through)

Date formats: july 7, jul 7, 2026-07-07, 7/7, 7/7/2026. Year defaults to the trip's start year if omitted.

Lane keywords: hotel / hotels / lodging, flight / flights, activity / activities, location / where. The word placeholder is tolerated, and a leading for or at before the title is stripped.
